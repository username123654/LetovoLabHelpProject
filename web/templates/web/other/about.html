About Letovo Lab Help

This website was created for the Letovo School IB DP Science department to simplify and centralize the process of creating and approving SEAF and RAF forms.

Forms created by a student are approved by a teacher, a lab assistant, a safety inspector and a department director (separately for SEAF and RAF forms). Non-student users will additionally have to be approved by a personnel member after the registration. There is also an "IT inspector" role to change users' passwords to Letovo Account ones.

To embed a form in Letovo Canvas, input the following as HTML code:
<iframe frameborder="1" src="[Paste the form URL here, without square brackets]" width="700" height="700"></iframe>

The website was created by a Letovo School student as an "ИВР" project. Report any bugs or suggest features to this email: letovolabprojecthelp@gmail.com (this is the address that is used to send equipment notifications)

What you're currently looking at is, most likely, not final, and will be updated at some point.
